My New House

This map shows the location of the Albert Heijn and Jumbo supermarkets in the Netherlands. The green dots represent houses for rent inside a 5 minute walk from the supermarkets. Red dots are other houses for rent outside the 5min distance

Please allow some time for the map to process the distance to each supermarketr

The map also shows the Life Barometer layer, which is a measure of the quality of life in different areas. The color scale ranges from red (low quality of life) to green (high quality of life).

Click on the supermarket or house icons to see more information about them.

The information is retrieved from the Funda and Pararius websites, which are popular real estate platforms in the Netherlands.
